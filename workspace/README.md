# workspace/

## What lives here
**Drafts and works-in-progress.** Anything skills produce lands here first. Nothing is "real" until you explicitly approve a save to its canonical location (e.g., `products/<x>/releases/<v>/prd.md`).

This folder also doubles as an inbox for raw notes, captured ideas, and pre-scoped research that doesn't yet belong to any release.

## When to use it
- Skills auto-save drafts here before asking to commit
- You manually drop notes, half-formed thoughts, or paste-from-meeting content here
- You can keep cross-release research here until it's ready to be scoped into a release

## When to clean up
Periodically. Nothing here is canonical, so it's safe to delete liberally. If a draft turned into something real, the skill should have moved it to its canonical location already.

## Privacy
Gitignored. Drafts and notes stay local.
